Further quality for everyone who enjoys coloring.
2 kinds of items to support a comfortable process and beautiful finish of hair coloring and to improve salon color quality.
By adding these 2 items to materia series which expresses clear and beautiful colors, more comfortable and more satisfying salon color will be achieved.
  • PRE SKIN CREAM
    150mL
    Protective cream for scalp
    (This product does not prevent skin rashes.)
    PRE SKIN CREAM protects your scalp from sensory irritation, itching, tightness, etc. caused by hair coloring, and supports the comfort of your scalp when treating hair.
  • SMOOTHING OIL
    500mL
    Emulsification aid
    SMOOTHING OIL ensures the hair is in a stable condition as it gently cleanses the scalp, enhancing comfort and color retention during the emulsification process.
